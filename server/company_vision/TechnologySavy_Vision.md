## 1. Mission Statement

TechnologySavy exists to build practical, high-impact software solutions that solve real problems for real people. We believe technology should be accessible, reliable, and designed with the user at the center. Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals by delivering smart, scalable products that create measurable value — without unnecessary complexity.

We are committed to moving fast, learning faster, and building a culture where innovation is driven by curiosity and grounded in execution.

---

## 2. Core Values

### 1. Ship Fast, Iterate Faster
We prioritize getting working solutions into users' hands quickly. Perfect is the enemy of good; we learn from real feedback and improve continuously.

### 2. User-First Thinking
Every decision we make starts with the question: "Does this make our users' lives better?" If the answer is no, we rethink it.

### 3. Radical Ownership
Every team member owns their work end-to-end. We don't pass blame — we solve problems, document lessons, and raise the bar for the next cycle.

## 4. Recommended Team Structure (5-Person Startup)

### Current Target Structure

| Role | Responsibility | Priority |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **CEO (You)** | Strategy, fundraising, partnerships, culture, final decisions | Filled |
| **Founding Engineer** | Architecture, backend/frontend development, technical leadership | **Hire First** |
| **Product Designer** | UX/UI, user research, prototyping, design system | Month 2–3 |
| **Full-Stack Developer** | Feature development, integrations, devops | Month 3–4 |
| **Growth / Operations Lead** | User acquisition, customer success, metrics, ops | Month 4–5 |

### Hiring Sequence

1. **Month 1:** Founding Engineer — This is the critical path. They will co-architect the MVP and set engineering culture.
2. **Month 2–3:** Product Designer — Needed once the core product direction is validated and we need to polish the experience.
3. **Month 3–4:** Full-Stack Developer — To accelerate feature velocity as we move from MVP to v1.
4. **Month 4–5:** Growth/Operations Lead — Once product-market fit signals emerge, we need someone to scale users and operations.

### Culture Notes
- **Flat hierarchy:** Everyone contributes ideas; the best idea wins.
- **Remote-first:** Hire the best talent regardless of location.
- **Weekly all-hands:** 30-minute sync on progress, blockers, and learnings.
- **No meetings Wednesdays:** Deep work day for the whole team.
