## Overview

| Milestone | Target Date | Phase | Key Deliverables |
|-----------|-------------|-------|------------------|
| M1 - Discovery Complete | Week 1 (Day 7) | Phase 1 | Research docs, personas, technical audit |
| M2 - Design Approved | Week 2 (Day 14) | Phase 2 | Wireframes, mockups, design system |
| M3 - Development Complete | Week 4 (Day 28) | Phase 3 | All pages built, CMS integrated |
| M4 - QA Passed | Week 5 (Day 35) | Phase 4 | Test reports, optimized build |
| M5 - Launch | Week 6 (Day 42) | Phase 5 | Live site, monitoring active |

## Critical Path

1. Discovery → Design approval → Development → QA → Launch
2. Any delay in design approval pushes entire development phase
3. CMS integration is a blocker for content review and launch

## Buffer Time

- 2 days buffer in Week 4 for integration issues
- 1 day buffer in Week 5 for critical bug fixes
- Total project buffer: 3 days
