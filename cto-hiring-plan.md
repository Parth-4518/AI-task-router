# CTO Hiring Plan

## Position Overview
**Role:** Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
**Company:** Paperclip AI
**Location:** Remote / Flexible

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead technical strategy and architecture decisions
- Manage engineering team and development processes
- Oversee AI/ML infrastructure and product development
- Drive technical innovation and R&D initiatives
- Ensure security, scalability, and reliability of systems

## Required Qualifications
- 10+ years of software engineering experience
- 5+ years in technical leadership roles
- Strong background in AI/ML systems and infrastructure
- Experience with distributed systems and cloud architecture
- Proven track record of building and scaling engineering teams
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills

## Hiring Process
1. **Job Posting** - Publish on LinkedIn, AngelList, and tech-specific boards
2. **Initial Screening** - Resume review and phone screen (30 min)
3. **Technical Interview** - System design and coding challenge
4. **Leadership Interview** - Team management and strategy discussion
5. **Final Interview** - Culture fit and vision alignment with CEO
6. **Offer & Negotiation** - Competitive package with equity component

## Interview Panel
- CEO (you) - Vision and culture fit
- Senior Engineer - Technical depth assessment
- External Advisor - Industry perspective

## Timeline
- Week 1-2: Job posting and initial applications
- Week 3-4: Screening and first-round interviews
- Week 5-6: Final interviews and decision
- Week 7: Offer and onboarding

## Compensation Range
- Base Salary: $200K - $300K (depending on experience)
- Equity: 0.5% - 2% (vesting over 4 years)
- Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401k match, unlimited PTO
